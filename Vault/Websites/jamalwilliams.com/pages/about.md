# About Me: The Sovereign Architect

### The Origin: Set by a Renaissance Man
I built my first website when I was 14. It was a sprawling, 38-page monstrosity with spinning email gifs and midi background music, but it was *mine*. 

Around that same time, I remember my mother calling my father a "Renaissance Man." When she explained what that meant—a person whose expertise spans a significant number of different subject areas—my path was set. I didn't want to be the "best" at one thing; I wanted to understand everything that was interesting.

I traded my paper route for a tech support job at Compuserve at 15, and I haven't worked a day since that didn't involve a computer. But while my childhood pursuits of coding and technology took the lead, a passion for photography was quietly growing on the sidelines.

### The Evolution: Snapshots to Lore
Visual storytelling has always been in my blood. I grew up in a family that treated photography as a sacred act. The photos displayed around our house weren't just objects; they were members of the family, accompanied by lore that turned snapshots into legacy.

In 2010, the spark caught flame. I rediscovered what my parents knew all along: a photo's true power isn't in its technical perfection, but in the story it tells. I spent over two decades in software development—building internal web applications and large-scale portals for companies like Dell—while simultaneously learning to capture the atmosphere, movement, and emotion of the human experience.

### The Architect: Life + Experience = Sovereignty
For years, I kept these lives separate. I was the "Tech Guy" in the boardroom and the "Creative" behind the lens. 

But life has a way of integrating you. Through twenty years of software architecture and fifteen years of professional photography, I realized that I wasn't just "doing" two different things. I was exercising the same muscle: **The muscle of Design.**

*   **Logic + Experience** led to Digital Sovereignty (The System).
*   **Art + Witness** led to Creative Sovereignty (The Story).
*   **Strategy + Community** led to Financial Sovereignty (The Life).

Today, I am a **Sovereign Architect**. I help brands find their voice, tell their story, and transform their businesses by building digital systems that actually work. I help individuals reclaim their identity through soulful, intentional portraiture. And I am building a legacy in Detroit, a city that—like me—is defined by its ability to reinvent itself through grit and design.

I am an unapologetic generalist. I make things better. **By design.**
