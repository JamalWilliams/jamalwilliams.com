### Freedom
I want the ability to choose my life and how I live it. That means ensuring I have good options at all times and I'm never in a situation where I'm forced to do the things I don't want to.

*   **In Practice:** Choosing where I live and work; building systems that provide leverage and time.
*   **The Shadow:** Dependency on a single source of income or location; lacking the resources to choose.

### Integrity
I want to do things in a way that I'm proud of. I want to engage in good faith and deliver on my word.

*   **In Practice:** Engaging in good faith; delivering on promises; doing things the right way even when it's harder.
*   **The Shadow:** Taking unnecessary shortcuts; agreeing to things I cannot do.

### Responsibility
I take ownership of the things in my life—both the good and the bad. Sovereign people don't make excuses; they make adjustments.
