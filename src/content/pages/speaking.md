Scaling a business is just another form of systems architecture. I deconstruct the "Growth S.T.O.R.Y. Framework" and show how the principles that run global e-commerce platforms can be applied to boutique brands.

Can a luxury boudoir studio run with 80% automation without losing its soul? I walk through the technical implementation of my n8n and GoHighLevel ecosystem and how it transformed JWI from a "job" into a "practice."
