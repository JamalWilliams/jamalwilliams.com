# JWI: Automating the Intimate

### The Challenge: The "High-Touch" Bottleneck
JW Intimates is a luxury boudoir studio where "The Slow Exhale" is the primary product. However, the administrative burden of scheduling, payments, and client nurturing threatened to drown the creative process in "The Hustle."

### The Solution: The Sovereign Automation Engine
I architected a custom ecosystem using **n8n**, **GoHighLevel**, and **OpenAI** to handle the noise so I could focus on the signal.

- **80% Administrative Reduction**: Automated the entire lead-to-shoot workflow.
- **Soulful Nurturing**: Used n8n to ensure every client receives personalized, timely communication without manual intervention.
- **Financial Sovereignty**: Integrated Stripe and GHL to ensure all payments are finalized before the client enters the studio.

### The Impact
By automating the "Intimacy," I reclaimed 20 hours a week for creative direction and strategy, proving that tech can be a bridge to more human connection, not less.
